MINDEF/SAF Speech Video Generator

Welcome to the MINDEF/SAF Speech Video Generator. This state-of-the-art AI product can improve productivity by allowing users to generate a video of them giving a speech in their own voice from a user-input script. The engine takes in the speech text, two 10-seconds voice clips of the user, and a 10 seconds video of the user facing the camera with a static background. We envision this product to help users quickly create presentation videos, speeches and lectures without reading long scripts and without re-recording due to errors during reading.
